# Excel 365 Alignment Audit

This checklist maps the Japanese objective to concrete artifacts in this repo.
It is not a claim of full Excel 365 pixel parity; it records verified coverage
and known gaps.

## Objective Breakdown

- Align UI/UX, menus, dialogs, ribbon design, and behavior toward Excel 365.
- Use web-sourced oracle information, including screenshots.
- Keep coverage verifiable through local tests and visual snapshots.

## Known Gaps

- Full Excel 365 pixel parity is not proven.
- Not every Excel menu hierarchy, Backstage page, dialog tab detail, responsive
  state, hover/focus/disabled state, theme, and platform/browser rendering has
  been implemented or visually compared.
- Chromium is the verified visual/E2E browser in this environment.
